ADVENTUREWORKS TIMELINE STRAND

*This timeline is to be used as a guide only.

  STEP 1

Pre Enrolment

  • Do you have the prerequisite personal skills and leadership experience required for this qualification?
  • Are you a NZ permanent resident or citizen?
  • Do you have a group you can practice your leadership skills with during the qualification?
  • Are you able to attend the three day training and 2-3 day assessment?
  • Can you participate in evening tutorials online?

  STEP 2

Enrolment

WEEK 0
  • Submit log book or other evidence of previous personal skills and leadership experience
  • Submit evidence of NZ citizenship or residency
  • We will create your NZOIA membership (you do not need to do this)

  STEP 3

Fundamentals Course

WEEK 1 - 12
  • Fundamentals online course. Self directed and teacher supported online learning

  STEP 4

Training Workshop

WEEK 5
  • Three day in person workshop usually delivered about week five

  STEP 5

Practice

WEEK 6-20
  • Self-directed - practice leadership and personal skills for the skills and leadership assessment

  STEP 6

Planning Assessment

WEEK 19
  • Finish leadership plan assessment

  STEP 7

Leader & Skills Assessment

WEEK 20
  • Attend personal and leadership in person assessment, leading a model client group

  STEP 8

Review & Evaluation Assessment

WEEK 21-24
  • Finish leadership review assessment

  STEP 9

Qualification Complete

  • Qualification loaded onto NZQA framework
  • NZOIA Award registered by AWL
